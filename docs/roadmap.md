# Aletheia Product Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Approved
**Release:** 0.1.0
**Sprint:** 0 – Foundation

---

# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term evolution of Aletheia.

Rather than listing features, it describes the capabilities the platform will acquire over time and the strategic direction guiding its development.

Each release builds upon the previous one while preserving the architectural principles defined in the Platform Architecture.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Aletheia is not intended to become another AI assistant.

It is designed to become an **AI Operating System for Knowledge Work**.

The platform will help individuals and organizations transform information into understanding, understanding into decisions, and decisions into measurable outcomes.

Every release should move Aletheia closer to this vision.

---

# 3. Product Evolution

The evolution of Aletheia follows a natural knowledge lifecycle.

```text
Foundation
        ↓
Knowledge
        ↓
Understanding
        ↓
Reasoning
        ↓
Decision
        ↓
Creation
        ↓
Learning
        ↓
Continuous Evolution
```

Each stage represents a new platform capability rather than simply adding new features.

---

# 6. Development Philosophy

Aletheia follows several guiding principles throughout its evolution.

* Build capabilities before features.
* Prefer quality over speed.
* Keep the architecture stable.
* Deliver incremental value.
* Design for long-term maintainability.
* Keep humans in control.
* Measure everything that matters.
* Learn continuously from feedback.

---

# 7. Success Indicators

Progress should be evaluated by capabilities rather than the number of implemented features.

Examples include:

* Knowledge quality
* Response accuracy
* Decision support effectiveness
* User productivity
* Content quality
* Platform reliability
* Maintainability
* User trust

---

# 8. Roadmap Governance

The roadmap is a living strategic document.

Minor implementation changes do not require roadmap updates.

Major capability additions or changes in product direction should be reflected in future versions of this document.

---

# 9. Conclusion

The roadmap is not a checklist.

It is the long-term evolution plan for Aletheia.

Every sprint, feature, and architectural decision should contribute to advancing the platform toward its vision of becoming an AI Operating System for Knowledge Work.
